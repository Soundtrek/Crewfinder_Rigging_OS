# Compliance Context

## Purpose
This document provides operational context for how Crewfinder Rigging OS should be designed for use in South African live events and entertainment production environments.

It exists to guide product design, workflow structure, and documentation support.

It does not provide legal advice, legal certification, or engineering approval.

---

## South African Operating Context
Crewfinder Rigging OS is expected to be used in environments where live event safety, temporary structures, rigging operations, contractor competency, and documented work practices matter.

In South Africa, the relevant operating context includes:
- occupational health and safety obligations
- live event safety practices
- temporary structure planning and verification
- documented equipment inspection and control
- site-specific risk assessment and method statements
- clear lines of supervision and responsibility

---

## Standards and Frameworks Referenced by Context
The system should be aware of, and compatible with, operational environments that refer to:
- Occupational Health and Safety Act context
- SANS 10366 event safety context
- venue-specific safety manuals and contractor requirements
- company internal safety procedures

This repository treats those as context for workflow design, not as a source for claiming compliance automatically.

---

## Workflow Implications
Because this product will be used in operational live-event environments, the product should support workflows that naturally fit with:
- feasibility review
- planning
- pre-production setup
- production-day checks
- post-production strike and closeout

---

## Documentation Implications
The product should be capable of supporting structured records such as:
- job records
- calculation records
- point verification logs
- equipment references
- inspection and check records
- signoff history
- attached supporting documents

---

## Product Rule
Rigging OS must help users organise and prove what they did.
It must not claim to prove that all legal obligations have been satisfied.
