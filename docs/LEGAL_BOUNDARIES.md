# Legal Boundaries and Safety Disclaimers

## Purpose
Define strict limits on what Rigging OS represents and prevents any misleading interpretation of outputs.

---

## Absolute Rules

The system must NEVER:
- claim legal compliance
- claim engineering approval
- certify structures or rigging setups
- replace qualified professional judgement

---

## Responsibility Model

Responsibility remains with:
- the employer
- the appointed competent person
- the supervising technical authority

The system is a support tool only.

---

## Output Classification

All outputs must be clearly understood as:
- calculated values
- advisory indicators
- workflow records

NOT:
- certified approvals

---

## Required Disclaimer Language (Conceptual)

All exports and reports must include a statement that:
- calculations are based on user inputs
- results are advisory
- verification is required by competent persons

---

## UI Implications

The interface must:
- clearly distinguish between safe / warning / critical
- not visually suggest approval or certification

---

## Audit Protection

System must:
- store all inputs
- store all outputs
- record user identity
- record timestamps

---

## Final Rule

Rigging OS helps organise, calculate, and record.
It does not approve, certify, or take responsibility.
